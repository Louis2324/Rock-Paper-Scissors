Rock-Paper-Scissors

Welcome to my Rock-Paper-Scissors game .
Like all games, it has rules to make it fair... or do they?

Game Rules

The winning rules are the same as regular rock-paper-scissors:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Additional twists:

  • Whenever you draw, both scores are deducted by one point.
  • If you draw three times consecutively, the player with fewer points wins.
